Pack-a-Punch

These steps must be completed four times, once for each God's temple.

Once entering a temple, go to the top floor and find the large gong. Ring the gong by interacting with it (F on PC), and a Champion (mini-boss) will appear. Once the Champion is dead, you must pick up its head.

With all four heads in hand, reach the lowest section of the map (Temple) and place them on the four spikes around the center altar. Once completed, the Pack-a-Punch will appear.

God Challenges

Directly after spawning into the map, you should proceed to begin these God Challenges. You must complete at least three in order to unlock the acid trap.

In order to begin the challenges, go to the entrance of one of the God Towers in the arena and locate the banner there. Knifing the rope next to the banner will assign the player to that God and begin the challenges. Note: You will see a message appear with instructions when you are looking at the rope.

Every time you complete a challenge, a reward will be spawned into your God's Bowl on the platform in the center of the arena. For each of the Power Ups, they will spawn on the ground next to the pedastal after interacting with the Bowl.

Rewards

  • Level 1: Heart. Provides the player with a single free revive (can revive themselves while down).
  • Level 2: Bonus Points. Provides the player with a 500 Bonus Point power up.
  • Level 3: Pack-a-Punched Strife (Z Harmony)
  • Level 4: Max Ammo Power Up
  • Level 5: Full Power Power Up. Restores your Special Weapon's charge
  • Level 6: LMG. A random, non-Pack-a-Punched LMG
  • Level 7: Extra Perk
    • An extra, fifth perk slot with a free random perk.
    • This perk will be lost when you are downed (unless revived by Rah's Sceptre)
  • Level 8: Max Ammo Power Up
  • Level 9: Pack-a-Punched Hades (Archeron Alliterator)

Acid Trap

The acid trap is a buildable trap that is used for several different Easter Egg sections. There are three components that must be picked up.

The three parts are always in the same locations:

  • Chain
    • Hanging off of a torch in the lower section of the Temple.
    • Note: You do not need to unlock the Pack-a-Punch to retrieve this item, only open the Temple.
    • This component is found on the opposite side from the Gear
  • Gear
    • Sitting on the floor against a wall in the lower section of the Temple.
    • Note: You do not need to unlock the Pack-a-Punch to retrieve this item, only open the Temple.
    • This component is found on the opposite side from the Chain
  • Bowl
    • On top of the pedastal in the center of the Arena
    • You must complete three God Challenges in order for this component to appear.

With all three pieces, you simply must go to one of the three Acid Trap locations and build the trap (similar to building the shield).

Trap Locations:

  • Zues Tower: On the stairs between the top floor and ground floor.
  • Odin Tower: On the stiars between the top floor and ground floor.
