Clocks and Symbols
Finding the Symbols

Once you have activated the Artifact for the first time, a series of Symbols will appear in four locations across the map. These locations are picked randomly from a pool of six locations.

In order to solve this puzzle, you must locate all four symbols, which are commonly refered to as Fire, Water, Air, and Earth. Once you find a symbol, you must located the associated clock, which is found nearby in the same room. If your memory is strong, you can try to memorize all the symbols and their associated times, or you can use the site you're on to keep a quick record.

Setting the Dials

Once you have found all four symbols, you must match several dials found around the ship to these clocks. You must go to either the Bridge, Poop Deck (artifact spawn), or Engine Room. At each of these locations you will find several radial levers that can be adjusted using the action key (F on PC). Adjusting to the right by aiming to the right side of the dial will increase the time by one interval. On dials using minutes, this is a 5 minute segment. On dials using hours, this is a 1 hour segment. Follow the associated guides below to solve the dials at each location.

Bridge

Inspect each of the levers found on the bridge carefully. You will notice there are symbols on 4 of the dials. These dials will represent the minute hands for each of the clocks. Simply set the proper dial to the minutes on the associated clock.

e.g. If your Fire clock was set to 3:55, move the fire lever one notch to the left.

Poop Deck

There are only two, unmarked dials found in the same spot the Artifact spawned (on top of a catwalk). The left dial is associated with the hour hand of the Air symbol. The right dial is associated with the hour hand of the Earth symbol. See this image

Engine Room

Directly down the stairs in front of Odin's altar, you will find the final two dials. While there are four dials on the pipes, only the bottom left and top right are moveable. The boottom left dial is associated with the hour hand of the Fire symbol. The top right dial is associated with the hour hand of the Water symbol.

Wrapping Up

You will know you have set all eight dials properly when you hear a loud, ominous sound and the dials can no longer be moved. You are ready to move on to the next step: Outlets

Outlets and Portals
Finding the Outlets

This step must be completed after the clocks step. If you have not completed the Clocks step, the outlets will not be activated.

There are six outlets hidden throughout the ship. During this section of the Easter Egg, four of them will be spewing different elements. One poison, one fire, one water, and one electricity. You may need to watch the outlets for several seconds in order to see the effect. Note: if you activate an outlet, all other outlets will go inert until the end of the round.

Activating the Outlets

In order to activate an outlet, you must kill a zombie of the appropriate type in front of it. For example, to activate the fire outlet, you must lure a fire zombie in front of the outlet, then kill it.

You will know you have activated the outlet when a large, orange portal appears on the ground in front of it.

Only one portal may be activated per round, and once a portal has been activated, all the other outlets will be inert until the round is finished. Once all four portals have been activated, you can proceed to the next section.

Entering the Portals

Once all portals have been activated, you will need to enter them in a particular order (Poison/Acid -> Water -> Electric -> Fire). To activate, all players must stand on the portal and hold their action keys/buttons (F on PC). Then, the players will be teleported to a certain part of the ship, with all the entrances sealed off. You will be locked in this area until all enemies have been slain.

Once all the enemies are dead, the doors will open and the Artifact will float within reach, allowing you to pick it up again. Do not forget to pick up the Artifact or you will have to start the portals over again.

Once all four portals are completed, you will be ready to enter the next step: Flooding the Engine Room.

Kraken

The Kraken is a two-barreled cannon that can be rewarded by the Mystery Box. It can be Pack-a-Punched once, but also be upgraded using the Distillery Kit in the Engine Room.


In order to receive a free Kraken, you must appease the Kraken chest. The chest will spawn in one of five locations. In order to open it, you must skill a Stoker and pick up the key he drops. Once you have the key, open the chest and begin killing zombies near it. Blue orbs will fly from a fallen zombie's body into the chest.

After a certain number of kills, the chest will be "appeased" and will close, then teleport to one of the other four locations. You must repeat this process two more times. Once complete, the chest will reveal an item. If the item is a compass, you must go to the Boat Deck outside of the Grand Staircase. If the item is a telescope, you must go to the Poop Deck near the Artifact spawn.

Once you arrive at the correct location after appeasing the chest, a large tentacle will drop a free Kraken onto a nearby box. Any player may pick it up. It does not depend on who opened or completed the chest.
