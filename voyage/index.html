Pack-a-Punch

Note: If this is your first time playing, I highly recommend getting aquainted with the ship first. You should know how to navigate from the spawn to the engine room without much difficulty before attempting the full Easter Egg.

When you spawn, you will find yourself on the front end (bow/Forecastle) of the ship. Your first goal will be to unlock doors until you reach the other end (stern/Poop Deck). Once there, you will see a glowing object suspended above a catwalk. Climb onto the catwalk and interact with the Artifact to open additional areas of the ship and begin the Easter Egg.

Four terminals will appear across the ship.

  • Poop Deck
  • Turbine Room
  • Grand Staircase
  • Cargo Hold
The first of these terminals will be located near where you grabbed the artifact. Interact with the terminal to unlock it. Once you have unlocked all four terminals, the Pack-a-Punch will appear in place of the last activated terminal. This location is not static, and will move from round to round to one of the four terminal locations.

Unlocking All Locations

Once you have an elemental Kraken, you may unlock the Pack-a-Punch in all locations. If you do not yet have one, you can follow the Kraken guide and Distillery guide.

In the engine room, use the Kraken to shoot the four moving pistons in the center of the room. They will begin smoking and stop moving.

Six blinking red lights will turn on around the room. Turn the wheel located near each light.

Once turning all four wheels, go to the Turbine Room and interact with the switch on the wall next to the entrance. The Pack-a-Punch will now be available in all four locations.

Distillery

The distillery is a buildable workbench that can be used to upgrade the Kraken to different elemental types. One of these types (acid) is necessary to complete the flooding section of the Easter Egg. There are three pieces in total, each with a unique set of spawn locations. Once all three are gathered, the distillery can be built above the Turbine Room on the wooden workbench.

Pipes

For this next section, you will need a Kraken upgraded with the Acid element. See the Distillery section for more info.

Once you have the restored Artifact, you will need to go to the Turbine Room. There will be 9 blue pipes with one or two leaks in them. Initially, these leaks will be spewing steam only. But, once you hit them with an Acid Kraken, they will also begin leaking small droplets of water. After all nine have been hit, the room will re-flood.

Next, you will need to wait until the Pack-a-Punch is moved back into the turbine room. You will then be able to Pack-a-Punch the Artifact, completing this step of the Easter Egg.

Ballistic Shield

The shield is a buildable piece of equipment that can both be used as a weapon, similar to Ajax's shield from multiplayer, and also as a protection to the player's back. There are three pieces that need to be located before it can be built. Each piece randomly spawns in 2 or 3 unique locations.

You can check your progress in finding the parts at any time by holding the TAB key on PC.

Upgraded Shield

Once the shield has been created, it can be upgraded via another series of steps. The upgraded shield has more health and a freezing bullet effect and is particularly effective against Blight Fathers

First, grab a shield, go to the Mail Room, and locate the medium-sized safe on the floor. You should notice a small, crab-like device on it - this is the code cracker from the Intro Cutscene. Bash the code cracker with the shield to cause it to fall to the ground and pick it up.

Next, look around the nearby rooms for four bones. Near each bone should be a piece of paper with a code on it. Take note of these four codes and which piece of the skeleton it is near.

With the code(s) in hand, you must locate four safes around the map.

  • 1st Class Lounge
  • Millionaire Suites
  • 3rd Class Berths
  • Birdge (Captains Quarters)
Place the code cracker on the safe and look at the code it generates. This code will correspond to a bone piece and its piece of paper. Return to the mail room, pick up the matching bone, and place it in the matching safe. If done correctly, the code cracker should be able to be removed from the safe again by hitting it with the shield.
Note: If you pick up the wrong bone, simply interact with the spot it was sitting to place it back down.

In order to do the next safe, you must complete the current round.

Once all four bones have been placed into safes, return to the cargo hold and look to the left of the portal at the bottom of the stairs. Interact with the car inside the crate and it will sink into the floor, then begin driving around in the sky. Having audio on can be helpful in locating the car, since it plays a unique sound while flying.

Next, you must navigate the map and shoot small green vials, similar to the ones seen in the Intro Cutscene. These vials are located within mail tubes, so look for long, curved piping when searching. The mail tubes will always be located within an interior window/broken door.

  • Bridge (2)
  • Aft Decks
  • 1st Class Lounge
  • 3rd Class Berths

Once you shoot a vial, it will drop out of another pipe in one of several locations.

  • Millinoaire Suites
  • Mail Room
  • Engine Room
Pick up the vial and add it to your inventory. You must retrieve one vials before proceeding, but may retrieve up to four.

Once you have a vial, place it within one of four small white tubes on the ship's deck.

  • To the right of Danu
Shooting the white tube after will cause a firework to shoot into the air. Use these fireworks to hit the car. If you miss, you may retry with the same tube after a cooldown period. The care may disappear after a certain time limit, and may be reactivated by reusing the car in the Cargo Hold.

Once hit, the car will fly around a bit more before running into an iceberg in the water. A blue piece of ice will fly towards the shield workbench and upgrade the shield. Your current shield will not be automatically upgraded, but picking up the new version is free.

Clocks and Symbols
Finding the Symbols

Once you have activated the Artifact for the first time, a series of Symbols will appear in four locations across the map. These locations are picked randomly from a pool of six locations.

In order to solve this puzzle, you must locate all four symbols, which are commonly refered to as Fire, Water, Air, and Earth. Once you find a symbol, you must located the associated clock, which is found nearby in the same room. If your memory is strong, you can try to memorize all the symbols and their associated times, or you can use the site you're on to keep a quick record.

Setting the Dials

Once you have found all four symbols, you must match several dials found around the ship to these clocks. You must go to either the Bridge, Poop Deck (artifact spawn), or Engine Room. At each of these locations you will find several radial levers that can be adjusted using the action key (F on PC). Adjusting to the right by aiming to the right side of the dial will increase the time by one interval. On dials using minutes, this is a 5 minute segment. On dials using hours, this is a 1 hour segment. Follow the associated guides below to solve the dials at each location.

Bridge

Inspect each of the levers found on the bridge carefully. You will notice there are symbols on 4 of the dials. These dials will represent the minute hands for each of the clocks. Simply set the proper dial to the minutes on the associated clock.

e.g. If your Fire clock was set to 3:55, move the fire lever one notch to the left.

Poop Deck

There are only two, unmarked dials found in the same spot the Artifact spawned (on top of a catwalk). The left dial is associated with the hour hand of the Air symbol. The right dial is associated with the hour hand of the Earth symbol. See this image

Engine Room

Directly down the stairs in front of Odin's altar, you will find the final two dials. While there are four dials on the pipes, only the bottom left and top right are moveable. The boottom left dial is associated with the hour hand of the Fire symbol. The top right dial is associated with the hour hand of the Water symbol.

Wrapping Up

You will know you have set all eight dials properly when you hear a loud, ominous sound and the dials can no longer be moved. You are ready to move on to the next step: Outlets

Outlets and Portals
Finding the Outlets

This step must be completed after the clocks step. If you have not completed the Clocks step, the outlets will not be activated.

There are six outlets hidden throughout the ship. During this section of the Easter Egg, four of them will be spewing different elements. One poison, one fire, one water, and one electricity. You may need to watch the outlets for several seconds in order to see the effect. Note: if you activate an outlet, all other outlets will go inert until the end of the round.

Activating the Outlets

In order to activate an outlet, you must kill a zombie of the appropriate type in front of it. For example, to activate the fire outlet, you must lure a fire zombie in front of the outlet, then kill it.

You will know you have activated the outlet when a large, orange portal appears on the ground in front of it.

Only one portal may be activated per round, and once a portal has been activated, all the other outlets will be inert until the round is finished. Once all four portals have been activated, you can proceed to the next section.

Entering the Portals

Once all portals have been activated, you will need to enter them in a particular order (Poison/Acid -> Water -> Electric -> Fire). To activate, all players must stand on the portal and hold their action keys/buttons (F on PC). Then, the players will be teleported to a certain part of the ship, with all the entrances sealed off. You will be locked in this area until all enemies have been slain.

Once all the enemies are dead, the doors will open and the Artifact will float within reach, allowing you to pick it up again. Do not forget to pick up the Artifact or you will have to start the portals over again.

Once all four portals are completed, you will be ready to enter the next step: Flooding the Engine Room.

Kraken

The Kraken is a two-barreled cannon that can be rewarded by the Mystery Box. It can be Pack-a-Punched once, but also be upgraded using the Distillery Kit in the Engine Room.


In order to receive a free Kraken, you must appease the Kraken chest. The chest will spawn in one of five locations. In order to open it, you must skill a Stoker (large fire zombie with a shovel) and pick up the key he drops. Once you have the key, open the chest and begin killing zombies near it. Blue orbs will fly from a fallen zombie's body into the chest.

After a certain number of kills, the chest will be "appeased" and will close, then teleport to one of the other four locations. You must repeat this process two more times. Once complete, the chest will reveal an item. If the item is a compass, you must go to the Boat Deck outside of the Grand Staircase. If the item is a telescope, you must go to the Poop Deck near the Artifact spawn.

Once you arrive at the correct location after appeasing the chest, a large tentacle will drop a free Kraken onto a nearby box. Any player may pick it up. It does not depend on who opened or completed the chest.
